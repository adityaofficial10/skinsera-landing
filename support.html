Support

Need help with Skinsera? Start with these FAQs.

1) How does the skin score work?

Skinsera’s AI analyzes your daily photo across five zones: texture, clarity, oiliness, redness, and consistency. Your score is on a 0–100 scale, and a lower score means better skin.

2) Why does my score go up sometimes?

Scores can fluctuate due to lighting, natural skin changes, and lifestyle variation. Focus on your trend over 2+ weeks instead of day-to-day movement.

3) How do I delete my data?

In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

4) Does Skinsera share my photos?

No. Photos are processed to generate your score and are never shared with third parties.

5) The app isn't working / I found a bug.

Email hello@skinsera.app with your device model and iOS version so we can debug quickly.

6) How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage subscriptions in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Skinsera.
